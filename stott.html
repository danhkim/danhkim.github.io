Graphic design

A quick, just for kicks, totally unnecessary redesign of Andy Stott's album Faith in Strangers.

Wanted to highlight through type and material the album's moments of alienation through heavily processed imagery. The texture for the disc was made out of screenshots collected of the blur effect applied to loading images on Medium articles and masked into outlines of egg shells.
